FAQ

Which vehicle do i ride in?

Due to insurance regulations in some provinces, you must travel in your own vehicle as the chase vehicle is not insured to take passengers

How does your service work?

Safe Designated Drivers is a designated driving company that sends you two drivers. One driver drives you home in your vehicle and the other follows to pick up the driver.

Can you take passengers in the other car??

Due to insurance liabilities passengers can only go in the customers car.

Do you take advance bookings?

Yes we do. Booking in advance is the best way to ensure you don’t have to wait for your safe ride home when you’re ready to go.

Why do I have to provide a credit card in order to book a designated driver?

We have to take a credit card in order to protect our drivers from showing up at a pick up destination only to find out the customer is no longer there. If we didn’t than that driver team would be out the time and gas it took them to go to that pick up location.

On a busy night if we took bookings without credit cards and people cancelled without notice the company would potentially lose out on other bookings, drivers wouldn’t earn any money and as a result this service couldn’t exist all year.
