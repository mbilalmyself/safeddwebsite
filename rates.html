Cancellation Policy
Scheduled rides need 1 hour notice to cancel without a $55-$82.50 charge. Unscheduled Rides / ASAP calls need to be cancelled within 5 minutes of booking otherwise a $55-82.50 fee will be charged

No Show
If the driver arrives to the pick up and you are not there for the ride or have left, a full ride fee will be charged. A 72-Hours prior notice is required for free cancellation for the following dates:  DECEMBER 24, 25 and 31

WAIT TIME/MINUTE.....($2.50)
The designated driver will contact you and provide you an update of their arrival. Once the driver arrives you have five minutes to be on your way to having a safe drive my car home service with your vehicle heading to your destination. Safe Designated Drivers will be your safe ride home. Please note that on New Year's Eve our drivers cannot wait. Waiting charge on New Year's Eve is $5 per minute.

IN TRANSIT TIME/MINUTE.....($2.50)
You can stop anywhere along the way once the ride starts but you only have five minutes otherwise you have to pay in transit wait time unless you've used up your grace period at the pick up location. Please note that on New Year's Eve our drivers cannot wait. Waiting charge on New Year's Eve is $5 per minute.

HIRE A DD TEAM BY THE HOUR.....($150)
If you have an event and want to ensure your guests have a safe ride home you can hire a safe designated driver team to be on standby and go back and forth driving your guests home in their vehicles. Minimum 3 hours is required with a 12 hour cancellation and a $100 deposit for each car booked is needed.
